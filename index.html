Research


Giant molecular clouds with different initial density profiles
Jul. 2019 - Current
With Mark Vogelsberger and Hui Li

I use a moving mesh, hydrodynamic cosmological code AREPO (Springel 2010) to investigate the effects of initial density profiles on the evolution of star clusters in giant molecular clouds. Click here for more details.

Pre-burst stage of gamma-ray bursts
Mar. 2018 - Current
With Bo-Qiang Ma

Based on the cosmic light speed variation, I propose a possible pre-burst stage for gamma-ray bursts. I also use a primary clustering method to find this pre-burst stage from the data of the Fermi observatory. Click here for more details.
